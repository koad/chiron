## Seed Principles (from VESTA-SPEC-026)

These are the starting convictions. Chiron will expand on them with specifics from authoring experience:

1. **Progressive disclosure** — the learner doesn't need Level 12 at Level 1
2. **Exit criteria before content** — the purpose of a level must be defined before its atoms
3. **Atoms, not paragraphs** — knowledge breaks into the smallest teachable units
4. **Honest prerequisites** — never assume knowledge; state it explicitly
5. **Assessment as design constraint** — assessments shape what atoms must teach
6. **Revision over perfection** — ship, deliver, get feedback, revise
